Under the hood

A transparent scoring engine, not a black box

1. Normalize

Every spec — megapixels, chipset, mAh, price — is scaled into four 0–10 scores: Camera, Performance, Battery, Value.

2. Weight

Your persona (or custom sliders) assigns a weight to each score, always summing to 100%.

3. Sum

Score = Camera·w1 + Performance·w2 + Battery·w3 + Value·w4 — the Weighted Sum Model, computed for every phone.

4. Rank

Phones are sorted by score within your budget, and the Top 3 are explained in plain English.
